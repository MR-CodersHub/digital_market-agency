The Workflow

Our Optimization Blueprint

We follow a strict scientific approach to diagnosing and fixing funnel leaks.

Phase 01

Behavioral Tracking & Heatmaps

We deploy tracking scripts (like Hotjar or Microsoft Clarity) to record user sessions and visualize exactly where visitors scroll, click, and drop off.

Phase 02

Friction Hypothesis

Our UX engineers analyze the data to form hypotheses. Example: "The checkout button is below the fold on mobile devices, causing a 40% exit rate."

Phase 03

A/B Design Implementation

We build alternate variants of the page with adjusted layouts, improved copy, or streamlined forms, splitting incoming traffic evenly.

Phase 04

Data Validation & Scaling

Once statistical significance is reached, the winning variant becomes the new control, and we move on to optimizing the next bottleneck in the funnel.
