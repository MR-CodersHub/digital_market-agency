FAQ

PPC Campaign FAQs

What ad spend budgets do you manage?

Our algorithms function best with sufficient data velocity. We typically manage accounts spending anywhere from $5k/mo up to multi-million dollar annual budgets.

Do you guarantee a specific ROAS?

While we can't guarantee exact figures due to market variables, our initial audits provide highly accurate forecasting models indicating expected CPA and ROAS based on current account history.

You do. You retain 100% ownership and admin control of your Google Ads, Meta Ads, and all historical data. We simply request manager access to perform the optimization work.
