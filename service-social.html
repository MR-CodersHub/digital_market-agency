FAQ

Social Marketing FAQs

Which platforms do you manage?

We specialize in the big four: Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and X (Twitter). However, our strategy dictates platform choice based entirely on where your specific B2B or B2C audience resides.

Do you create the video content?

Yes, we have an in-house creative team for editing, motion graphics, and scripting. We can either shoot on-location, use creator networks, or edit raw footage provided by your team into high-performing short-form content.

How is ROI tracked from social?

We use specialized UTM tracking, custom promo codes, and pixel integrations to map social interactions directly to sales pipelines, giving you a transparent cost-per-acquisition metric.
