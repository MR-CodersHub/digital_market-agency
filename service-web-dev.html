FAQ

Web Development FAQs

Why Jamstack over traditional WordPress?

Jamstack decouples the frontend from the backend database. This means your site loads instantly via CDN, is practically immune to database hacks, handles traffic spikes effortlessly, and avoids the bloated plugin issues of WP.

How easily can I update content?

Very easily. We integrate intuitive Headless CMS dashboards. Your team can publish blogs, update products, or change copy exactly like a traditional CMS, and it rebuilds automatically.

Do you migrate existing websites?

Yes. We handle full migrations, including transferring database schemas, redirecting old URLs to preserve SEO juice, and moving content to the new headless architecture.
